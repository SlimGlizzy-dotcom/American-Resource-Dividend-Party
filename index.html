Building a Future for All Americans

The American Resource Dividend Party believes America’s natural wealth should benefit every citizen. By responsibly managing national resources, we can provide healthcare, education, infrastructure, and economic security for the American people.

“America is rich. The people deserve a share.”

10 Point Platform

1. Resource Ownership

America’s natural resources should benefit the people directly.

2. Basic Income

Create a national dividend funded through resource profits.

3. Universal Healthcare

Guarantee healthcare access for all Americans.

4. Free Education

Tuition-free colleges, trade schools, and public education support.

5. Fair Economy

Strengthen workers, families, and small businesses nationwide.

6. Energy Independence

Invest in domestic energy and renewable innovation.

7. Anti-Corruption

Remove corporate influence from public resource management.

8. Housing Support

Expand affordable housing and stronger communities.

9. Infrastructure

Modernize roads, utilities, internet, and transportation systems.

10. Transparency

Ensure public accountability and open financial reporting.

Our Mission

We believe America’s natural wealth belongs to the people. Our mission is to transform resource profits into opportunity, stability, healthcare, education, and prosperity for every citizen.
